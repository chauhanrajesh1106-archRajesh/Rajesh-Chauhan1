Interoduction of Sent Andrew College

There are several institutions named St. Andrew's College; one is St. Andrew's College of Arts, Science and Commerce in Bandra, Mumbai, founded by the Catholic Archdiocese of Bombay in 1983, as seen on the college website. Another is St. Andrew's College Gorakhpur, founded by the Church Missionary Society in 1828 as a school that became a degree college in 1899, according to its official website. A third is St. Andrews Institute of Technology & Management (SAITM) in Gurgaon, which is affiliated with Maharshi Dayanand University (MDU), says Shiksha.
